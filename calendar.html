CASE STUDY

How the Project was Built

From strategy to execution, here's how I designed and built a welcome flow experience that drives results.

Overview

This project focused on building a monthly email campaign calendar for Wilde, a healthy snack brand targeting health-conscious consumers looking for high-protein alternatives.

The Problem

The brand needed a structured campaign plan that could support promotional goals while keeping subscribers engaged through more than discount-focused messaging.

The Approach

I created a monthly campaign strategy that balanced promotional campaigns, educational content, and seasonal opportunities within a consistent email cadence.

Execution

  • Planned a full August email campaign calendar around seasonal and promotional opportunities
  • Organized campaigns around product education, promotions, and audience engagement
  • Incorporated Back-to-School, National Sister's Day, and Tough Mudder into campaign themes
  • Balanced value-driven content with promotional messaging to support long-term engagement
  • Structured a 3-4 email per week cadence to maintain consistent communication

Impact

  • Created a more organized and strategic monthly campaign structure
  • Supported stronger audience engagement through content variety and cadence planning
  • Reduced reliance on discount-heavy messaging with educational campaign concepts
  • Built a scalable campaign framework for future seasonal and promotional planning
