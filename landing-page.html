CASE STUDY

How the Project was Built

From strategy to execution, here's how I designed and built a landing page that drives results.

Overview

This project focused on building a lightweight landing page connected to ConvertKit to support subscriber growth through a simple and conversion-focused signup experience.

The Problem

Creators and small brands often need landing pages that not only look clean but also connect seamlessly into real email marketing systems and automations.

The Approach

I designed a minimal landing page experience focused on clarity, responsiveness, and a direct connection between front-end interaction and email automation.

Execution

  • Built a responsive landing page using HTML and CSS
  • Designed a clean layout focused on a single conversion action
  • Created and configured a live ConvertKit signup form
  • Embedded the form directly into the page and connected submissions to automations
  • Styled the signup experience to feel fully integrated with the overall design

Impact

  • Created a production-ready landing page connected to a real marketing platform
  • Improved clarity and usability through focused messaging and layout structure
  • Demonstrated front-end development combined with marketing system integration
  • Built a reusable framework for future campaigns, newsletters, and lead generation
