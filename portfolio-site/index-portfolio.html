About

I started my career as an Email Developer and spent three years coding responsive, cross-client emails using HTML, CSS, and a bit of JavaScript. During that time, I wanted to understand the bigger picture — how each part of the digital experience connects — and began exploring ways to expand my skills and bring more value to the teams I work with.

That curiosity pushed me to take courses in Figma, earn email marketing certifications from CXL and HubSpot, learn how to build sites in WordPress, and build custom Shopify themes with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Liquid. Those projects helped me grow from building emails to creating full web experiences.

Outside of work, I enjoy working out, playing video games, and watching my favorite content creators react to random videos — anything from sports highlights to “what if the dinosaur extinction never happened.” My favorite game of all time is Madden — my friends and I used to set up tournaments back in college, and we still talk about those nights to this day. I’ve always been big on friendly competition and finding ways to push myself — and whether it’s sports, games, or the web, I love how they bring people together and how much you can grow when you’re around a great group of people.
